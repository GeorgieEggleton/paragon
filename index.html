The Crew

Owain

Ex Royal Navy wepons engineering submariner officer turned mad robotics scientist. Owain learned to sail with his parents from a young age and bought Paragon nearly 10 years ago as his floating that could be moved with him as he transferred bases in the Navy. Now out of the Navy and working for a robotics company his plans are now to use skills to make Paragon as self sufficant and eco-friendly as possible.

Georgie

Ex professional sailor Georgie has spent many years sailing all over the world but returned to the UK a few years ago. Now studying to become a full stack web developer and preparing Paragon for long term life at sea. With 6 trans-atlantics under her belt as well as tens of thousands of sea miles Georgie is keen to set sail on Paragon on her first full circumnavigation.

Tilly
